Spaghetti Bolonese

Today we'll be making an amazing Spaghetti Bolognese. Originating from Italy.

Ingredients

  • 100g Dried Pasta
  • 400g 5% Lean Mince
  • 500g Dolmio Sauce

Method

  1. Boil the pasta to al dente
  2. Cook the meat and add the sauce
  3. Once both are done, add the pasta to the meat.
