Calamari

Today we'll be making an amazing Calamari. Originating from Italy.

Ingredients

  • 100g Flour
  • 400g Squid Rings
  • 10g Salt

Method

  1. Mix the flour and the salt.
  2. Add the squid rings into the bowl. Make sure they all have an even coat
  3. Deep fry squid rings until golden brown.
